# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Streetwear shoppers browsing a fashion storefront on mobile first, then desktop. They are looking for new drops, collection campaigns, product rails, and a premium brand feel before buying.

## Product Purpose

Create a Lao and English fashion ecommerce storefront inspired by premium campaign-led streetwear sites. Success means the page feels close to the reference in scale, pacing, typography, and product presentation while staying ready for future login, stock, admin, and dashboard features.

## Brand Personality

Sharp, cinematic, restrained.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic ecommerce templates, oversized marketing cards, decorative gradients, warm beige SaaS palettes, and UI that feels unrelated to the Represent-style reference.

## Design Principles

- Lead with full-bleed campaign imagery and let the image carry the page.
- Keep controls minimal, centered, and consistent across repeated campaign sections.
- Make mobile sizing the source of truth, then scale up to desktop.
- Preserve the existing English storefront font and use Lao text with a Lao-capable font.
- Prefer precise spacing and sizing over extra decoration.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Maintain readable contrast on image overlays, keep tap targets large enough on mobile, support reduced motion, and keep language switching clear for Lao and English users.
